Our Story

Built for Gardeners, by a Gardener

GardenCalc is a free gardening tools site built by a designer and gardener based in Texas. It started as a personal project to make accurate planting information easier to find — no fluff, no accounts, just useful calculators for real gardeners.

Currently covering Texas, New Mexico, and Oregon with more states coming soon.

What We Stand For

Our Values

The principles that guide every tool we build and every article we write.

Always Free

Great gardening knowledge should be accessible to everyone regardless of budget. Our core tools will always be free.

Accuracy First

We source data from NOAA, USDA, and university extension programs. We update calculations when science evolves.

Sustainability

We promote organic practices, companion planting, and soil health because a healthy garden starts with healthy soil.

Accessibility

Every page is mobile-responsive, screen-reader friendly, and optimized so it loads fast even on slow connections.

Community-Driven

Our best features come from gardener feedback. We read every email and implement suggestions that help the most people.

Privacy Respected

We don't sell your data. ZIP codes entered in our tools are never stored or associated with any personal information.
