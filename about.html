Our Story

Born from a Garden Journal and a Spreadsheet

It started the way most side projects do — with a problem. Our founder, an avid home gardener in Ohio, kept a battered spiral notebook full of hand-calculated planting dates, frost warnings pulled from weather apps, and rough companion planting notes scribbled in the margins.

Every spring she'd spend hours re-deriving the same information. She built a Google Sheet to automate it, then shared it with her local garden club. Within a month, hundreds of gardeners had copied it and started asking for more.

That spreadsheet became GardenCalc — a free, easy-to-use website that brings together the most useful gardening calculations in one clean, mobile-friendly place. No account required. No ads cluttering up your results. Just the answers your garden needs.

Today GardenCalc serves gardeners in all 50 states and across USDA Hardiness Zones 3 through 11. We add new tools and plant data every season based on feedback from our growing community.

What We Stand For

Our Values

The principles that guide every tool we build and every article we write.

Always Free

Accuracy First

We source data from NOAA, USDA, and university extension programs. We update calculations when science evolves.

Sustainability
